We’re thrilled to share our latest product and company news. Thank you to our network of physicians, clients, investors, video game mavens, and thought-leaders who have rallied behind our mission of unleashing skill-enhancing video games onto the global medical community.

Featured at the American College of Cardiology 2019 annual meeting, our 4th game, Cardio Ex, reimagines fluoroscopy—presenting visually stunning cases that test cardiologists’ skills in challenging scenarios: aortic stenosis, coronary artery perforations, thrombosis, and more.


Physicians can explore fully interactive virtual patients and deploy an arsenal of realistic interventional tools: guidewires, stents, balloons, atherectomy drills, contrast dye, and C-arm imaging—general cardiology and electrophysiology cases coming soon. CME credits are available to physicians for completing cases.


Get the full scoop in this month’s Diagnostic and Interventional Cardiology — Video Game Format Used to Train Cardiologists: Cardio Ex reimagines CME and training for practicing cardiologists.
